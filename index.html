Video

Basic recommendations

Handbrake is free open-source software to convert and compress video

  • Keep video below 30 seconds
  • Aim for under 10MB size
  • Encoding
    • A WebM container using the VP9 codec for video and the Opus codec for audio.
    • An MP4 container and the AVC (H.264) video codec, ideally with AAC as your audio codec
  • Convert to 720p
  • Remove audio
  • Lower frames per second (FPS) to 24-30
  • Reduce content quality

Attributes

autoplay
starts the video on page load. This often will not work unless the muted attribute exists. Will cause the video to download as soon as possible, so the preload attribute is redundant
muted
The video will play muted. Most browser will not allow autoplay sound unless the user has interacted with the page.
loop
loop the video (continuous play)
playsinline
Historically has been required for video to play in iOS. I haven't tested it recently
controls
show video controls
